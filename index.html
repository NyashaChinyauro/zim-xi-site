About The ZIM XI

The ZIM XI is a fan-driven platform dedicated to covering the Zimbabwe Premier Soccer League (ZPSL). Our mission is to bring Zimbabwean football to life with dynamic, accessible, and up-to-date coverage of everything happening in the league.

Whether youre a die-hard supporter, a casual follower, or a curious newcomer, this site is designed to keep you in the game with:

  • Fixtures & Results: Full-season match schedule and outcomes, updated weekly.
  • League Table: Real-time standings with points, goal difference, and form.
  • Team Profiles: Clickable club bios featuring history, top scorers, and form.
  • Player & Manager Stats: Top performers in goals, assists, clean sheets, and tactics.
  • News: Live coverage and curated updates from @TheZIMXI on X (Twitter).
  • Gallery: Visual highlights capturing matchday moments and fan culture.

Our content is curated with passion and accuracy by local football enthusiasts, media partners, and contributors. We aim to promote local talent, celebrate our clubs, and give Zimbabwean football the digital platform it deserves.
